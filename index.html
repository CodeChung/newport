CodeChung is a development shop that is excited about finding creative, impactful solutions that empower local businesses. Using today's trusted modern technologies including React and Node.js, we design websites for clients from scratch, focusing on quality and individuality.

Furthermore, with my own past experience in education, I pride myself in teaching clients the tools that they can use to quickly update their website from the comfort of their own homes. You can easily do it at your own convenience once we go over the steps together -- whether a custom Node.js API or a user-frienly CMS provider (Wordpress/Contentful), we effectively discuss how to take advantage of our online technologies to improve the website experience. With my guidance, you will develop the keys to your own kingdom; no more waiting for simple picture and blog post updates and no more extraneous fees for minor changes. Now that's power to the people.

In today's era of online marketing, it can be hard to find a way to differentiate yourself from others and truly find an online home where you can show the world who you are and what you do. I enjoy working one on one with business owners to find that unique, customized web experience that will leave a lasting impression on potential customers and connections.
